Automation Maturity Model

Level 1: Manual Workflow

Tasks are handled manually with little structure, documentation, or automation support.

Level 2: Organized Workflow

Processes are documented, responsibilities are clearer, and repeatable steps are easier to identify.

Level 3: Automated Workflow

Key repetitive tasks are automated using connected tools, alerts, forms, and workflow triggers.

Level 4: AI-Assisted Workflow

AI helps summarize, draft, classify, enrich, and prepare work before human review.

Level 5: Agentic Automation System

Connected automation systems move work across tools while keeping human judgment in the right places.
